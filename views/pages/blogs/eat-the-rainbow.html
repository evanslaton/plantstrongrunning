Eating The Rainbow

I know you know the slogan "Taste the Rainbow" and are probably imagining Skittles falling from the sky. Is it safe to assume Skittles spent hundreds of thousands of dollars on marketing so that this would be exactly what we all think about when we hear the phrase "eating the rainbow." I can't say that I am going to recommend a diet full of this particular rainbow but the term "rainbow" does have some significance when choosing foods that will work for you in the way you want it to.

Eating a diet with variety isn't only going to be more interesting for your taste buds; you're also getting a full spectrum of vitamins, minerals, and nutrients that are unique to red foods, green foods, orange foods, etc. (This is what you'll hear referred to as micronutrients, antioxidants, etc.) Eating a rainbow illustrates that we can learn about our food simply by looking at it.
