Coaching Programs

Your personalized program will radically improve your health and happiness. Together, we will explore concerns specific to you and your body and discover the tools you need for a lifetime of balance.

In my health coaching programs you:

  • Set and accomplish goals in a way that is empowering and exciting
  • Work to achieve and maintain your ideal weight
  • Understand and reduce your cravings
  • Increase your energy levels
  • Feel great in your body
  • Learn about new foods and how you can easily incorporate them into your diet
  • Improve your personal relationships
  • Discover the confidence to create the life you want

Your health coaching program includes:

  • Two 50-minute sessions per month
  • Email and text support between sessions
  • Recipes that are healthy and simple to prepare
  • Coaching and support to help you make the dietary and lifestyle changes you want
  • Simple but informative handouts that will increase your nutrition knowledge
  • My personal commitment to your health and success

Health History

In this one-hour break-through session we'll take a look at your eating and exercise habits, set goals and learn how to make sustainable changes so you can live the healthy life you've always wanted. I'll send you detailed notes of our conversation and follow-up with you twice to provide further support.

Ongoing Support

A great program if you're eager to live a healthy life but need help or struggle with self-control. All of my resources and knowledge will be at your disposal including my delicious recipes. We'll chat every other week to celebrate your progress and move on to the next step.

Intro to Running

Go from your couch to a running pro in this beginner program. You will learn all there is to know about running including injury prevention and proper nutrition so you have the energy to perform at your peak. Through our regular sessions we'll create a custom training schedule designed around your needs and goals.

Race Specific Training

Conquer any race from a 5k to a full marathon and beyond with a training schedule created specifically for you. We'll start by defining your goals, gauging your current fitness level and choosing a race. Then, during our sessions I'll provide the support you need to make sure you stay on track.

Maintenance Plans

If you are wanting to stay fit, get stronger or just need a kick in the rear to stay focused during your off season then a custom maintenance plan is what you need. After discussing your objectives we'll create your custom training schedule and touch base regularly to ensure you meet your goals.
