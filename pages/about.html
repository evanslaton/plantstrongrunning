Coach Stephanie

At twelve years old I announced that I was going to be a vegetarian for no other reason than wanting to carry the label. I wouldn’t call myself an animal-lover per-se, but rather perplexed by unnecessary death just so I could eat. I gave up my cheese burgers and swapped it for cheese pizza, faux chicken nuggets, and a foolish number of bagels with cream cheese. Healthy would be the last word to describe my lifestyle.

In my early 20’s I learned how to prepare a simple salad and when I realized I had never tried a brussel sprout before. It was also when I’d officially would call myself a runner, having completed my first marathon. It wasn’t until my mid to late 20’s when I started to think about the foods I was using as fuel and what that meant. I began reading and watching documentaries all about food and running and a common theme continued to pop up: plant based eating. Understanding the health benefits, I adopted this plant-based diet not even thinking twice about giving up my beloved cheese. I felt great.

Becoming a more serious runner and learning how to eat healthier was the catalyst to my career change. I stumbled upon the Institute of Integrative Nutrition which not only inspired the beginnings of Plant Strong Running, but also endorsed the concept of Food As Medicine.

We all have our own unique health journeys and our stories are the most important place to start before we can make healthy sustainable changes. If you are ready to tell your story I encourage you to schedule your initial health history session with me where I’ll offer recommendations designed exclusively for you and you can learn more about working with a health coach.

I am a certified holistic health and marathon running coach and provide ongoing one-on-one programs that focus on examining your day-to-day life to get you back the energy and confidence you desire.

As your coach, in running and in life, I help you create your plant-strong lifestyle in the most delicious, stress-free, and healthy way. I work with you to make sustainable lifestyle changes you need to live the active life you envision and help make it a reality.
