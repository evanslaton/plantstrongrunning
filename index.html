Why Plant Strong?

If there were a food filled with nutrients, vitamins, minerals, and everything else we needed to live a vibrant, healthy and happy lives would you commit to eating only that? What if it was full of flavor and could be prepared in multiple ways so that you never got sick of it? Growing up many of us were taught to clean our plates and that we need to eat meat for protein, but if you begin to learn about diet and nutrition you begin to learn that these lessons aren’t fact; the lesson you learn is to eat more vegetables.

As health and wellness become your focus and goal, eating more plants must be priority. Eating well will support you in becoming the strongest and best self you can be. Whatever your reason for wanting to eat a plant based diet- for health, animals, or the environment- eating plant strong is rewarding and invigorating. With the right resources, recipes, and food hacks, a plant-based or vegan diet is exciting as you discover new foods and recreate old favorites. As your coach, in running and in life, I’ll help you create your plant-strong lifestyle in the most delicious, stress-free, and healthy way.

Coaching Programs

When I began to introduce more whole fruits, vegetables, beans and legumes to my diet I started to feel better and was more excited about food and health. I felt stronger running, my recovery time decreased, and I developed a newfound sense of clarity about myself and my environment. Something even more exciting: I rarely got sick. With each delicious meal I eat, I know I’m leaving a lighter footprint on the earth and I’m keeping my heart happy knowing I’m doing my part in saving countless animal lives. With my customized plans for clients, I support you each step of the way on your new journey to a healthier, happier, and plan-strong self.

* Each program is customizable to your specific needs and all coaching can be done remotely *

Health History

Over the course of an hour you will have an in-depth discussion on your health history. Coach Stephanie will help you set health goals for yourself and you'll receive a full set of notes detailing the conversation.

Ongoing Support

Offered in monthly, 3-month, and 6-month packages, this program includes the Health History where you will set health goals for yourself. You'll have two 50-minute appointments with Coach Stephanie each month to keep you on track. You will also receive books, handouts, recipes and other materials.

Intro to Running

In this 3-month program you will learn the basics of running and have regular access to Coach Stephanie. This is in addition to a customized training schedule designed around your specific needs and goals.

Race Specific Training

A personalized program for your upcoming race. Your customized training schedule is designed around your specific needs and goals and you'll have regular access to Coach Stephanie.
